# Changelog

## 0.1.1

- Tested with NVDA 2026.1 and updated add-on compatibility metadata.
- Added a legacy SAPI 5 preset fallback to try `sapi5_32` when a stored `sapi5` voice is not available in the 64-bit SAPI 5 driver.

## 0.1.0

- Initial public release of Voice Switcher for NVDA.
- Save, load, rename, and delete speech voice presets from a simple dialog.
- Designed for blind and low-vision NVDA users.
